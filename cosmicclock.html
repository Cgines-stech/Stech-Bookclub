Cash's Final Notes

I enjoyed the book. It made me stop and think about how data can stick with people. Normally when I work with data, I put something on an X and Y axis and let the information between them tell the story.

This book helped me realize that you should actively shape the story so it becomes memorable for the audience rather than expecting the data to speak entirely for itself.

The only improvement I can think of is that the book could have expanded more into visual data. I tend to find visualizations much more compelling and effective at communicating a story, so it felt like either a missed opportunity or perhaps a topic meant for another book.
